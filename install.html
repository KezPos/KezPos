Step-by-Step

How to install KezPos
on your device.

1

Download the app

Tap the download button above for your platform. Android users get an .apk file, Windows users get a .exe installer.

2

Android: Allow installation from unknown sources

Since KezPos isn't on the Play Store, Android needs your permission to install it. When prompted, tap "Allow from this source" or go to:

General path (most phones)

Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps → Your browser → Allow

⚠️ You can turn this off again after installing for extra security.
💻 Windows users: just double-click the .exe and follow the setup wizard — no extra steps needed.
3

Open and install the file

Android: open your Downloads folder, tap KezPos.apk, then tap "Install". Takes about 10 seconds.

Windows: run KezPos-Setup.exe and follow the installer prompts. KezPos will appear on your desktop and Start menu.

4

Open KezPos and create your account

Launch KezPos from your home screen or desktop. Enter your email and shop name to create a free account. Your data syncs to the cloud automatically across all your devices.

✓

You're all set!

Add your first product, make your first sale, and watch your business data come alive. Sign in on any device with the same email and everything restores automatically.

✨ Pro tip: Export your database from Settings → Backup & Data as an extra safety net.

Android: Brand-specific settings

The exact path to enable "Unknown Sources" varies by phone brand:

📱 Samsung (One UI)

  • Settings → Apps → tap top-right menu (⋮) → Special access
  • → Install unknown apps → Chrome/Files → Allow

📱 Tecno / Infinix / itel (HiOS / XOS)

  • Settings → Privacy & Safety → Install unknown apps
  • → Select your browser or Files app → Enable

📱 Xiaomi / Redmi (MIUI)

  • Settings → Additional settings → Privacy → Install unknown apps
  • → Select your browser → Allow from this source

📱 Nokia / Stock Android

  • Settings → Apps & notifications → Advanced
  • → Special app access → Install unknown apps → Allow

📱 Huawei (EMUI)

  • Settings → Security → More settings
  • → Install apps from external sources → Allow

FAQ

Common questions.

Is KezPos really free?

KezPos is priced to be accessible for local businesses. One affordable monthly plan covers everything — cloud sync, all features, Android & Windows, and future updates. No hidden fees, no paywalls.

Why isn't it on the Play Store?

KezPos is in active development and distributed directly so updates reach you faster. It's safe — check our Privacy Policy for details on what data we collect (spoiler: very little).

What if I lose my phone or PC?

No problem. Sign in with the same email on any new device and all your data — products, customers, sales history — restores automatically from the cloud.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. KezPos is offline-first on all platforms. Make sales, add stock, record transactions — all without a connection. Everything syncs automatically when you reconnect.

Can I use it on both my phone and PC?

Absolutely. Install on both, sign in with the same account, and your data syncs between them. Manage inventory on your PC, make sales on your phone — it all stays in sync.

Which Bluetooth printers are supported?

Standard 58mm and 80mm Bluetooth thermal printers. Most affordable models sold in Kenya work fine. The app scans for nearby printers automatically when you connect.
