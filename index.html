    Quick Sales

    Search as you type, select, done. KezPos auto-calculates the total, tracks change as the customer hands you cash, and handles debt sales, cart discounts, and per-item discounts — without slowing the queue.

    Customer Credit

    Save a customer's name and number once. Every debt sale is logged to their profile. Find them by name or number at checkout — no notebook, no guessing who owes what.

    Offline-First, Auto-Synced

    No internet? Keep selling. KezPos stores everything locally and syncs automatically every 10 minutes when you're back online. Your data is never stuck on one device.

    Reports That Actually Tell You Something

    Hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly breakdowns. See profit, discounts, refunds, and stock value — filtered however you need. Export a detailed PDF or CSV covering your full inventory, debtors, and financials anytime.
