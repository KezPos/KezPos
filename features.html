    Infrastructure

    Enterprise-grade tools.
    Zero enterprise price.

    Bluetooth Printing

    Wireless support for standard 58mm and 80mm thermal receipt printers. Fast, no cables needed — print receipts and PDF reports directly.

    Offline-First, Cloud-Always

    Works 100% offline during power or internet outages on both Android and Windows. Automatically syncs to Supabase cloud once you're back online. Zero data loss.

    Secure Authentication

    Supabase-powered secure login. Your password is never stored in plain text. Enterprise-grade auth on a shopkeeper's budget.

    SQLite Database Backup

    Manually back up and restore your full local database anytime from Settings → Backup & Data. Your data, always in your hands.

    Full Data Portability

    Export your entire business history at any time — CSV, PDF, or database backup. You own your data — KezPos makes it easy to take it with you.

    Multi-Device Sync

    Sign in on any Android phone or Windows PC and your full business — products, customers, sales — restores automatically from the cloud.
