Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 Effective: June 2025 Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

KezPos is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what your rights are.

1. Introduction

KezPos ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

This policy applies to all users of the KezPos mobile application and is compliant with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the KezPos service:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account
  • Shop name — used to personalise receipts and reports
  • Sales and transaction data — your inventory items, customer records, receipts, and payment history that you enter into the App
📍 Location permission (Bluetooth printers only): Android requires location permission to scan for nearby Bluetooth devices when connecting a receipt printer. This permission is used solely to discover Bluetooth printers in your vicinity — we do not record, store, or transmit your physical location at any time.
✅ We do not collect device contacts, camera access, or any other sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your KezPos account
  • Sync your business data securely across devices
  • Restore your data when you sign in on a new device
  • Send important account and service notifications
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns (anonymised)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • Locally on your device — for offline access and performance
  • Supabase cloud servers — encrypted and securely hosted, used for backup and cross-device sync

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication to protect your information.

🔒 Your password is never stored in plain text. All authentication is handled securely through Supabase Auth.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete all your data at any time directly from the App:

  • Go to Settings → Backup & Data → Delete All Data
  • This permanently deletes all your data locally and from our cloud servers

You may also request account deletion by contacting us at kezposapp@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request erasure of your personal data
  • Portability — export your data at any time using the App's Export Database feature
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kezposapp@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

KezPos uses the following third-party service to operate:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication provider. Your data is processed in accordance with Supabase's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or any other third-party data processors.

8. Children's Privacy

KezPos is designed for business use and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you via the App or email if the changes are significant.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact:

KezPos Data Controller
📧 kezposapp@gmail.com
